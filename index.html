About Us

Whenever people come together, share a common vision of goal, put together their strengths and compliment each other, an organization grows to its greatest heights” In order to carry out a positive action, we must develop a positive vision here. With a vision of developing something for the greater good, we, the second year students of IIIT Bhubaneswar present before you ‘TEAM ZENITH’, a team of multitalented and skilled people who are passionate coders, amazing problem solvers and most importantly have a phenomenal understanding among them as a team. The aim of our team is to utilize our skills in order to visualize, analyze, and develop solutions to miscellaneous problems in the virtue of technology. We always try to build a feeling of oneness, of dependence on one another, and of strength to be derived by unity among the team members, so that each member of our team gives his cent percent in the allround progress.
